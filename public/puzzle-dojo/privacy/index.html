The short version

Arrow Puzzle Dojo: Tap Maze does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share

any personal data. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking,

and the game does not connect to the internet.

What the app stores

Your game progress (unlocked levels, star ratings, best times, daily-challenge

streak, and the sound on/off setting) is saved only on your device, using

the operating system's standard local app storage (iOS UserDefaults /

Android SharedPreferences via Unity PlayerPrefs). This data:

  • never leaves your device;
  • is not accessible to us or to any third party;
  • is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

What the app does NOT do

  • No personal information is collected (no name, email, contacts, location,

identifiers, or advertising ID).

  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are included.
  • No advertising SDKs are included and no ads are shown.
  • No in-app purchases.
  • No network calls are made by the game.
  • No data is shared with third parties.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected from anyone,

no data is collected from children, consistent with COPPA and GDPR-K.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA and similar laws)

Because we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to access, export,

correct, or delete. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored progress.

Third-party services

The app is built with the Unity engine. All optional Unity services

(Analytics, Ads, Cloud Diagnostics, In-App Purchasing) are disabled in

this app, and the release build performs no engine telemetry network calls.

Changes to this policy

If a future update adds features that change any of the above (for example,

opt-in analytics or ads), this policy will be updated first and the effective

date revised.
