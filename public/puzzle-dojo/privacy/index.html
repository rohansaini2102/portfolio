Puzzle Dojo — Privacy Policy

How we handle your information

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Puzzle Dojo ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game (the "App"). By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Puzzle Dojo is designed to be played without creating an account. We aim to collect as little information as possible:

  • Gameplay & device data: anonymous information such as level progress, high scores, in-app settings, app version, device model, and operating system, stored on your device.
  • Usage & diagnostics: aggregated, anonymized analytics and crash reports that help us fix bugs and improve the game.
  • Advertising identifiers: if the App shows ads, our advertising partners may access a device advertising ID to serve and measure ads.

We do not ask for your name, email, or contacts, and we do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve gameplay and features.
  • To save your progress and preferences on your device.
  • To diagnose crashes and understand aggregate usage trends.
  • To display and measure advertising, where applicable.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services that collect information used to identify you only as required to operate their features (for example, analytics, crash reporting, and advertising). These may include services such as Google Play Services, Google AdMob, and analytics providers. Each provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

4. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with applicable laws such as COPPA and GDPR-K. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

5. Data Retention & Security

Progress and settings are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App or clearing its data. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any data processed on our behalf, though no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

  • You can reset or limit ad personalization in your device settings.
  • You can clear locally stored game data by uninstalling the App.
  • Depending on your region, you may have rights to access or delete data — contact us to make a request.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
